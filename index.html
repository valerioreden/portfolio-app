Background

I am a game developer based in Philippines. I work with gameplay and graphics side of games. I implement tools to improve my workflows during development. I graduated senior high at STI College Malolos. There I was introduced to programming and discovered a whole new world where I can learn, play, and create fun stuffs using my own hands.

I began my self learning journey as I enrolled in college. After some time, I decided to take a break from school and pursue my dream to become independent. I was able to become a developer through my passion for learning and dedication to my goal.

As an independent developer, I work with other people mostly students who wants help with their projects through freelancing. If I am not working with my client, I am working on honing my skills in Game Development where my passion is. Currently, I only have one shipped game which is a thesis game I worked on with my client. Most of my games are prototypes or game jam entries used to learn how to solve exciting problems that comes up during game development.
